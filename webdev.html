Why Web Development?

Web Development is a critical skill in the digital age. It encompasses both front-end development (building the visible parts of a website) and back-end development (managing data and server processes). It enables the creation of dynamic, interactive websites that cater to users' needs. Whether you're building a blog, an e-commerce site, or a corporate webpage, the skills learned in web development are indispensable.
